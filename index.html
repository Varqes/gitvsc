Rocket Raccoon

Rocket Raccoon is a fictional character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.

Created by writer Bill Mantlo and artist Keith Giffen, the character first appeared in Marvel Preview #7 (Summer 1976). He is an intelligent, anthropomorphic raccoon, whois an expert marksman, weapon specialist and master tactician.

His name and aspects of his character are a nod to The Beatles' 1968 song "Rocky Raccoon".

Rocket Raccoon appeared as a prominent member in the 2008 relaunch of the superhero team Guardians of the Galaxy.
